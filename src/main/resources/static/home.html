You have to make full horizontal lines with the different shaped blocks that fall into the game area. Full lines will then disappear and provide points. The more lines you make at the same time, the more points you earn.


Left arrow key: move block to the left
Right arrow key: move block to the right
Down arrow key: move block down
Up arrow key: rotate block clockwise

Tetris is primarily composed of a field of play in which pieces of different geometric forms, called "tetrominos", descend from the top of the field. During this descent, the player can move the pieces laterally and rotate them until they touch the bottom of the field or land on a piece that had been placed before it.

On the other hand this version of the game implement a multiplayer mode, in this way two players can play at the same time to compete and see who is the best.

Finally and very important, this game was developed by three students of the Escuela Colombiana de Ingenieria Julio Garavito, Nicolas Cardenas, David Antonio Diaz and Pedro Mayorga.
